What is Lexis?

Lexis is a leading provider of legal research and analytics solutions built to streamline and enhance the research process for legal professionals, business analysts, and law enforcement agencies. By combining robust content assets such as court materials, jury instructions, and extensive practice libraries with versatile products like Lexis+, Practical Guidance, News with Factiva, Verdicts & Settlements, and CourtLink, Lexis empowers users to:

  • Accelerate Legal Research: Access reliable, up-to-date legal content and case histories with ease.
  • Enhance Decision-Making: Use data-driven insights and comprehensive analytics for sound legal and business decisions.
  • Simplify Workflows: Integrate seamlessly into everyday legal and research processes through intuitive interfaces and advanced tools.

How Lexis Works

Lexis leverages sophisticated search algorithms, natural language processing, and smart data analytics to deliver rapid and relevant results. Here's how it drives your research:

  • Intuitive Search: Use natural language queries that translate complex legal concepts into precise search results.
  • Advanced Filters: Refine searches with smart filters that highlight key data points from an extensive repository.
  • Real-Time Processing: Receive instant updates on case developments and new content as they become available.
  • Contextual Recommendations: Benefit from tailored suggestions that align with your search history and ongoing research interests.

Getting Started with Lexis

Ready to begin your journey with Lexis? Follow these easy steps to get started:

  1. Sign Up and Create Your Account: Visit the Lexis registration page and complete the sign-up process. Verify your email address to finalize account activation.
  2. Explore the Lexis Dashboard: Log in to access an intuitive dashboard featuring navigation menus, search bars, and personalized data visualizations. Familiarize yourself with the various sections tailored to your research needs.
  3. Run Your First Search: Enter a query using natural language in the search field to retrieve pertinent documents and case studies. Utilize advanced filtering options to hone in on critical content like court materials or jury instructions.
  4. Customize Your Experience: Configure personalized dashboards reflecting your chosen metrics and preferred content categories. Set up automated alerts and notifications to receive timely updates on new content and case developments.
  5. Leverage Specialized Tools: For comprehensive legal research, explore Lexis+ for AI-driven insights. Use Practical Guidance for step-by-step legal strategies and access Verdicts & Settlements for detailed case outcomes.
  6. Collaborate and Share: Invite colleagues to join your workspace for collaborative research. Utilize sharing tools to distribute findings and annotations across your team securely.
  7. Access Further Support: Visit our Help Center for detailed guides, video tutorials, and expert best practices. Contact our support team for personalized assistance.
